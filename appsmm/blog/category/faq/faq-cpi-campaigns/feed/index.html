When you promote a mobile application with installs, you choose between CPI and keyword installs. CPI installs is paid traffic that gives revenue and in-app activity immediately. Keyword installs give positions in search, so they become the source of organic traffic from the App Store or Play Store.

]]> What are the advantages of using CPI campaigns? https://keyapp.top/blog/faq/advantages-of-using-cpi-campaigns/ Wed, 15 Dec 2021 10:43:09 +0000 https://keyapp.top/blog/?p=4395 One of the main advantages to use CPI campaigns for new apps is the possibility to get the keywords into the index. As you may know, usually new apps on Google Play are not indexed for the keywords at once after publishing. You can set CPI campaign for your new app in low CPI countries which may help to get into the index not only in the geos you get installs from, but in other regions as well. 

CPI campaigns will be also helpful if you need to increase the number of downloads on the app listing page (for example, to get 5000+ instead of 1000+).
