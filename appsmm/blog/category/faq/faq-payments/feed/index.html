What should I do in such a case? https://keyapp.top/blog/faq/balance-do-not-change/ Fri, 13 Dec 2019 08:59:56 +0000 https://keyapp.top/blog/?p=2985 Please, wait up to 30 minutes after you top up the balance. If payment hasn’t been reflected, contact our support team in the open channel, Skype, Telegram or Whatsapp.

If you paid with a credit card, please check your email inbox as well, as we process all payments via certified services wayforpay.com and Stripe. They can require additional information from you to confirm the payment. 

If you paid by PayPal and your payment hasn’t been reflected in your account, please check the payment status and contact our support team so they could check the payment as well. Sometimes PayPal sets payments on hold before adding it to account balance.

]]> How to pay? https://keyapp.top/blog/faq/how-to-pay/ Fri, 13 Dec 2019 08:58:16 +0000 https://keyapp.top/blog/?p=2981 You can add money to your keyapp.top account using the following payment methods:

  • PayPal;
  • Binance Pay;
  • Bitcoin;
  • Credit card;
  • Alipay;
  • Payoneer;
  • Bank Transfer (valid for payments from 1000$).

Remember, that you can’t start the promotion with null balance. After you press the «Start promotion» button, the required amount of money will be withdrawn from the balance.

*Note that from 1.11.2018 PayPal conditions have been changed and you have to add 3,9% in addition to your payment summary as a special transaction fee.
