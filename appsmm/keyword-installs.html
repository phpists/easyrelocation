What is keyword installs?

Let's start with the definition. A user opens an App Store or Play Store and type in any keyword ("money saver", for example) and get search results. It is called search results by keyword "money saver". When one picks an app from the search result by this keyword and installs it, it is called keyword install. Keyword installs show the tendencies for Google or Apple algorithms.

They indicate that users get what they want, when they install your app after searching by a certain keyword. If your app is searched plus downloaded after it more often than any other app on the Store, you deserve to be in the Top positions for the exact search. So we provide keyword installs for the region you want to rank in the top and by search requests you need.

Getting app downloads regulary

When you get keyword installs for iOS or Android product, search robots analyze how many installs you received and combine this data with other metrics of your app. The main metrics for the algorithm will be the relation between app downloads to keyword searchers. So, when users tend to download your app more often after searching for a certain keyword you will be ranked for the next search higher.

Google algorithm analyses conversion rate from your app page (conversion into installs from a keyword search) a bit slower than the same from App Store. It will take up to 3 days for Google Play robot to react to our incentive installs. Contrary, iOS ranks change dynamically and each install may change the app position up or down in seconds.

To get app installs regularly you need to be searched TOP 1 or at least in TOP 2-5 for the crucial keywords of your keyword cloud. That means you always search for new keywords for your cloud and delete those you are in the TOP but do not receive the conversions you need. Pick the most promising keywords and promote them. Try to keep your main keywords in TOP 1, analyzing them at the Appsmm.ru cabinet. Run 1-2 days campaigns to push up those that have lost their positions.
