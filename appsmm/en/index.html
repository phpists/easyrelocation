Find out how to promote your app starting with these answers first:

🌳 You can promote applications doing ASO (app store optimization) and buying traffic for it. When you promote a mobile application with installs, you choose between CPI and keyword installs. CPI installs is paid traffic that gives revenue and in-app activity immediately. Keyword installs give positions in search, so they become the source of organic traffic from the App Store or Play Store.
🔑 Keyword promotion is promotion of your application in search for particular words and phrases people use to find your product at the Google Play or App Store. Think of having a racing game app. People will search for "racing game". And when they see your app in the top 5 for this phrase, they install it. Appsmm.ru platform promotes mobile applications to be seen in that top 5 positions.
🏆 New applications do not necessarily have keywords they can be found for. But you can make them be seen for the keywords first. Then, promote it by each of these keywords. With Appsmm.ru platform you can find the keywords you need to promote your mobile app for. Use our Smart Campaign tool for that.

  1. A keyword suggestion tool that allows you to develop a keyword cloud for your mobile app by entering a few initial keywords and then using the "+Suggest" button to receive more suggestions.
  2. A free rank, score, and traffic checker for individual keywords, as well as a keyword rank history tool for tracking the performance of your keywords over time. Keyword rank history is available in "My keywords" menu.
  3. A Smart campaign feature that allows you to discover the keywords your app is ranked for in a specific region.
